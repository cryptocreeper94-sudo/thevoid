## Packages
framer-motion | Complex animations for the recording interface and transitions
lucide-react | Icons for the UI
clsx | Conditional class names
tailwind-merge | Merging tailwind classes safely

## Notes
The app requires microphone access.
Backend handles STT (Speech-to-Text) and text generation.
Audio is sent as base64 string.
Visual style is dark, high-contrast, and expressive.
